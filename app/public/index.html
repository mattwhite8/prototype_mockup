Disconnect Spark Plugs

No one wants to see sparks flying in the middle of mower maintenance. Disconnect the spark plug to prevent the mower from kick-starting accidentally during the rest of the maintenance process. Remove the spark plugs and squirt about an ounce of oil into each spark plug socket. Reinstall the plugs but leave the plug wires disconnected.

Quick Tip: Your old spark plug may be in good condition, but for a few dollars a new one will perform better come spring.
